PointsToVolumeListInterface.AppendPoint(position, radius, velocity)¶

Adds a point to the point list that will be converted to a volume.

Parameters:
  • position (Union[maxon.Vector, c4d.Vector]) – The position of the point.
  • radius (float) – The radius of the volume sphere that will be created around that point.
  • velocity (Union[maxon.Vector, c4d.Vector]) – The velocity of the points for trails.
